Name:
Spanish Rice with Avocado
Type:
Vegetables
Country:
Spain
Author:
Internet Cooking Conference 
Ingredients:
1 tb Butter or margerine 
1 tb Olive oil 
1 sm Onion, finely chopped 
1 Clove garlic, finely minced 
1 c Uncooked rice (I used basmati) 
1/4 ts Salt 
1/4 ts Dried oregano, crushed 
1/4 ts Ground cumin 
1/4 ts Ground tumeric 
1 cn 14 1/2 oz chicken broth, or 1 3/4 chicken stock 
1 sm Avocado 
Instructions:
Place butter and oil in 2-quart pan over medium heat. When butter is melted, add onion and garlic; cook until onion is tender. 
Add rice; cook, stirring constantly, 3 minutes or until rice looks milky and opaque. 
Add salt, oregano, cumin, tumeric, and chicken broth. Bring to a boil. cover; reduce heat and simmer 20-25 minutes or until rice is tender and all liquid is absorbed. 
Peel and pit avocado; dice. Fluff up rice with fork; add avocado and toss gently. Turn off heat
Serves:
6
Notes:
I have also made this with peeled, seeded, diced tomatoes instead of avocado.
