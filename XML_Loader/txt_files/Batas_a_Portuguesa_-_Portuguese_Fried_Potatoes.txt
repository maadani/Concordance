Name:
Batas a Portuguesa - Portuguese Fried Potatoes
Type:
Vegetables
Country:
Portugal
Author:
?
Ingredients:
1/4 c Butter 
1/4 c Oil (olive oil may be used) 
750 g Potatoes, peeled and sliced 1/4" thick rounds 
1/2 Level teaspoon salt 
1/2 Freshly ground black pepper 
1 Level tablespoon finely chopped parsley 
Instructions:
1. in a pan heat butter and oil. fry potatoes on both sides until golden brown. 2. season potatoes with salt and pepper, transfer to a heated plater and serve immediately sprinkle with parsley
Serves:
4
