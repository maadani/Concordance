Name:
Portuguese Sauce
Type:
Sauces
Country:
Portugal
Author:
Cita Bonds of Concord, California 
Ingredients:
1/2 c Chili suace 
1/2 c Catsup 
1/2 c Tomato juice 
1/4 c Chopped dill pickles 
1/4 c Chopped celery 
2 ts Capers 
3 tb Chopped onions 
2 Cloves garlic 
2 tb Stuffed green olives, chopped 
1/2 ts Tabasco sauce 
2 tb Worchestershire sauce 
1 ts Seasoned instant tenderizer 
Instructions:
Combine all ingredients in a blender. Blend on highest speed for about 5 minutes. 
Makes about 2 cups
Serves:
2
Notes:
This comes from my Dad's oldest sister.
