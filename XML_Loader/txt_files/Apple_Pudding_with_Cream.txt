Name:
Apple Pudding with Cream
Type:
Cakes
Country:
Nederland
Author:
Unknown (Internet)
Ingredients:
1 lb. sour apples
3 C. water
3/4 C. sugar
1/2 grated lemon rind
1 T. gelatin
1 C. cream

Instructions:
Cook apples in water with lemon rind. Strain, add sugar and dissolved gelatin. When cool add whipped cream. Pour into mold and chill.
