Name:
Ham Divan
Type:
Pork
Country:
USA
Author:
Great American Recipes
Ingredients:
4 slices, about 1 pound baked ham 
2 packages (10 ounces each) broccoli 
Sauce:   
2 tablespoons butter or margarine 
2 tablespoons all-purpose flour 
1/2 cup chicken broth 
1/2 to 3/4 cup half-and-half 
1 tablespoon prepared mustard or 2 teaspoons dry mustard 
Dash ground nutmeg 
1/2 cup shredded Swiss cheese 
1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese 
  Yellow or red bell pepper, for garnish, optional 

Instructions:
1. Cook broccoli according to package directions. Drain well.

2. Prepare sauce: In a saucepan, melt butter. Add flour and stir to combine.

3. Add chicken broth and half-and-half in a steady stream, stirring all the time while heating. Cook until sauce thickens.

4. Add mustard, nutmeg and shredded Swiss cheese.

5. Place ham slices in a shallow baking dish.

6. Top with broccoli.

7. Spoon sauce over. Sprinkle with Parmesan cheese.

8. Bake at 400 degrees for 15 to 20 minutes until cheese is melted and dish has a light brown color.

9. Garnish with bell pepper, if desired. Serve.

Serves:
7
