Name:
Cinnamon-Spiced Tea
Type:
Drinks
Country:
Thailand
Author:
Morten
Ingredients:
8 Chinese-Style Red Or Black Tea Bags Or: 
1/4 c Regular Black Tea 
4 c Boiling Water 
Ground Cinnamon 
1/2 c Sweetened Condensed Milk 
Instructions:
Place tea in a teapot; pour water over tea. Let steep for 5 minutes. Pour into 4 cups. To each serving, add a dash of ground cinnamon and about 2 tablespoons of condensed milk; stir to blend
Serves:
4
