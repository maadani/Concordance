Name:
Minced Lamb Kebabs
Type:
Lamb
Country:
Australia
Author:
Unknown (Internet)
Ingredients:
400g minced lamb 
1 onion grated 
1/4 tsp salt 
1 egg, beaten 
1/8 tsp cinnamon 
1/8 tsp ground cumin 
1 tsp lemon rind 
1 recipe Tomato Yogurt Dressing 

Instructions:
Combine all ingredients except dressing, mix well. Divide into 8 portions, form into small sausage shapes. Place two shapes onto each skewer. Place under hot griller, turn frequently. Grill 5 - 7 minutes. Serve with Tomato Yogurt Dressing.

Serves:
4
