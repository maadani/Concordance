Name:
Four Cheese Pizza
Type:
Pizza
Country:
Italy
Author:
Sandi
Ingredients:
1 large (12 inch) or 4 small (6 inch) Boboli Italian bread shell(s) 
5 ounce pouch Boboli pizza sauce 
1 ounce ricotta cheese 
1 ounce Parmesan cheese, grated 
1 ounce Kraft natural shredded cheddar cheese 
3 ounces Kraft natural shredded mozzarella cheese 

Instructions:
Spread pizza sauce then ricotta cheese on top of bread shell. Sprinkle cheeses over sauce in order given. Bake at 450 degrees for 12 - 15 minutes (5 - 8 minutes for 6 inch shells).
