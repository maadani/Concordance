Name:
Egg Soup (Sopa de Huevo)
Type:
Soups
Country:
Mexico
Author:
http://mexico.udg.mx
Ingredients:
3 eggs 
6 cups of chicken broth 
1 tablespoon of flour 
1 pinch of chopped nuts 
1 teaspoon of chopped parsley 
salt and pepper 
Instructions:
Whip the eggs with the flour. 
Boil the broth and then gently strain the egg mixture into the broth. Allow it to cook on low flame 10 minutes. Add the nuts, salt, pepper and chopped parsley
