Name:
Saus Kacang
Type:
Sauces
Country:
Thailand
Author:
Morten
Ingredients:
Pan drippings 
1 Thai chile 
1/2 Onion 
1 Garlic clove 
1/4 ts Shrimp paste 
3 1/2 tb Peanut butter, crunchy 
1 ts Salt 
3 tb Tamarind water 
1 c Coconut milk 
Instructions:
Grind together pepper, onion, garlic, & shrimp paste. Fry together in a little vegetable oil until the onion is golden. Add the pan drip- pings. Add the peanut butter, salt, and tamarind water. Stir and cook, adding coconut milk, until the sauce is the consistency of thick gravy.
