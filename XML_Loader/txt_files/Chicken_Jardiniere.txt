Name:
Chicken Jardiniere
Type:
Poultry
Country:
USA
Author:
Recipes Plus
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon vegetable oil 
2 pounds chicken pieces, skin removed if desired 
2 cups water 
1 1/2 teaspoon dried tarragon 
1 package Knorr Onin Soup Mix 
2 cups thinly sliced carrots 
2 cups broccoli florets 
2 cups quartered mushrooms 
3/4 cup light or regular sour cream 
2 tablespoons corn starch 

Instructions:
In large skillet over medium heat, heat oil. Add chicken; brown on both sides. Drain off fat.

Add water and tarragon; stir in soup mix. Cover and simmer 25 minutes.

Add vegetables; bring to boil. Reduce heat, cover and simmer 5 minutes. Remove chicken to platter.

Mix sour cream and corn starch; stir into vegetable mixture. Bring to boil; cook 1 minute, stirring constantly. Spoon sauce and vegetables over chicken. Serve with rice or noodl

Serves:
6
