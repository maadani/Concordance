Name:
Calypso Cooler
Type:
Drinks
Country:
USA
Author:
http://www.topsecretrecipes.com
Ingredients:
1 1/4 ounces Captain Morgan spiced rum
1/2 ounce peach schnapps
4 ounces (1/2 cup) orange juice
splash Rose's lime juice
1/2 ounce grenadine

Garnish
orange wedge
maraschino cherry
Instructions:
1. Fill a 16-ounce glass with ice.
2. Pour all ingredients over ice in the order listed. Don't stir. 
3. Garnish with an orange wedge and a cherry on a toothpick. Serve with a straw. 
Serves:
1
