Name:
Grilled or Broiled Pork Chops
Type:
Pork
Country:
USA
Author:
Gourmet Gold Premium Pork
Ingredients:
Pork chops 
  Pepper, to taste 
  Spices, to taste 

Instructions:
1. Season pork chops with pepper and spices, but do not salt.
2. Grill uncovered over medium coals or on a gas grill on medium-high, or grill broil 3 - 4 inches from element until pork is golden brown, 7 - 8 minutes per side.
3. For thicker or thinner cuts, adust cooking times as needed.
