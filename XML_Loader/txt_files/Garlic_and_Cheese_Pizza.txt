Name:
Garlic and Cheese Pizza
Type:
Pizza
Country:
Italy
Author:
Sandi
Ingredients:
1 Platina cooked pizza crust 
1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, grated 
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese, grated 
1/2 cup cheddar cheese, grated 
3 cloves  crushed garlic 
1/4 pound butter 
  Fresh basil 
  Toasted pine nuts 

Instructions:
Blend together the cheeses, garlic and butter. Spread a thick layer over Patina.

Bake in oven 15 minutes at moderate temperature (350 - 375 degrees) until cheese melts.

Sprinkle with basil and toasted pine nuts.

Serve with soup or salad.
