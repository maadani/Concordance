Name:
South-of-the-border burgers
Type:
Meat
Country:
USA
Author:
http://www.chatelaine.com
Ingredients:
1 lb (500 g) ground beef 
1 egg, lightly beaten 
1/4 cup (50 mL) salsa 
1 1/2 cups (375 mL) grated cheddar or packaged grated cheese, such as mozzarella-cheddar mix 
1/4 tsp (1 mL) each of salt and freshly ground black pepper 
Instructions:
Grease grill and preheat barbecue to medium-high. In a medium-size bowl, mix beef with egg, salsa, cheese, salt and pepper just until combined. Shape into 4 burgers. Place burgers on grill and barbecue until thoroughly cooked through and no pink remains in centre, at least 5 to 6 min. per side. Makes 4 burgers

Nutrients per patty 
30.8 g protein, 28.6 g fat, 1.6 g carbohydrates, 2.5 mg iron, 328 mg calcium, 528 mg sodium, 0.3 g fibre, 392 calories
