Caramel-Pecan Pie
Type:
Pies

Author:

Ingredients:
1 9-inch unbaked pie shell
2/3 cups sugar
1/4 cup butter or margarine, melted
3 eggs
1 jar (12 ounces) Smucker's Caramel Topping
1-1/2 cups pecan halves

Instructions:
In mixing bowl, beat eggs slightly with fork. Add sugar, stirring until dissolved. Stir in topping and butter; mix well. Stir in pecan halves. Pour filling into pie shell. Bake at 350Â° F for 45 minutes or until knife inserted off-center comes out clean.

Cool thoroughly on rack before serving. Cover; chill to store.

Serves:
1
