Name:
Avocado Soup
Type:
Soups
Country:
Mexico
Author:
Morten
Ingredients:
14 1/2 oz Chicken broth 
2 md Avocados 
1 Thin slice onion 
3 ts Lime juice 
1/2 ts Salt 
1 1/2 c Milk 
1 Lime 
Instructions:
In a blender, combine chicken broth, avocados, onion slice, lime juice, and salt. Cover the blender and blend unntil mixture is smooth, pour into a bowl. Stir in milk. Cover and chill thoroughly. Serve with lime wedges.
