Name:
Low Cal Curried Rice
Type:
Vegetarian
Country:
Thailand
Author:
Syd's Cookbook
Ingredients:
LOW FAT COCONUT MILK: 
18 oz Non Fat Plain Yogurt 
8 oz Non Fat Milk 
2 Packets Equal Sweetener 
2 ts Imitation Coconut Extract 

CURRY: 
3 c Low Fat Coconut Milk 
1 tb Thai Yellow Curry Base 
1/2 lb Mushrooms, Sliced 
3 c Minute Rice 


Instructions:
Prepare the imitation coconut milk, or use the real stuff. Place the coconut milk, mushrooms and the curry base in a sauce pan and warm to just before boiling. Add the Minute Rice and allow to stand for 10 minutes. This is a low fat version that makes a nice side dish
Serves:
4
