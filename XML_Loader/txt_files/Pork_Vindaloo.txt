Name:
Pork Vindaloo
Type:
Pork
Country:
India
Author:
Unknown (Internet)
Ingredients:
Pork, cubed 1 kg. 
Red chilies 10 nos. 
Peppercorns  6 nos. 
Ginger  1" piece 
Garlic  10 cloves 
Cinnamon  1" piece 
Cloves 6 nos. 
Vinegar  1 cup  
  
  

Instructions:
1.Make a masala by grinding the spices, ginger, garlic with vinegar. 
2.Apply the masala to the pork pieces and marinate the pork for 2 days in the fridge. 

3.After two days cook with the lid on, until meat is tender. 

4.Cook the pork without any water.

5.If you want it more dry then simmer till the gravy becomes more thick and dry.
