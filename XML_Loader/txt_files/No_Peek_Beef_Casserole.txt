Name:
No Peek Beef Casserole
Type:
Cassoroles
Country:
USA
Author:
Sandi
Ingredients:
2 pounds stew beef, cut into 1 inch pieces 
1 3/8 ounce envelope dry onion soup mix 
1/2 cup red wine 
10 3/4 ounce can cream of mushroom soup 
4 ounce can whole mushrooms, drained 
1/4 cup quick-cooking tapioca (optional 

Instructions:
Combine all ingredients in crock-pot. Stir together well. Cover and cook on Low 8 to 12 hours. Serve over noodles or rice. (High: 4 to 6 hours). 
Serves:
4
