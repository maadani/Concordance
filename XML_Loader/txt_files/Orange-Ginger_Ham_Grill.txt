Name:
Orange-Ginger Ham Grill
Type:
Barbeque
Country:
USA
Author:
Better Homes and Gardens
Ingredients:
1/4 cup frozen orange juice concentrate, thawed 
1/4 cup dry white wine 
1 teaspoon dry mustard 
1/4 teaspoon ground ginger 
1 1 1/2 to 2 pound fully cooked ham slice, cut 1 inch thick 
6 canned pineapple slices 
 Orange slices (optional 

Instructions:
Combine orange juice concentrate, wine, mustard, and ginger. Slash fat edge of ham slice. Brush sauce over ham. Grill over medium coals for 10 to 15 minutes, brushing with sauce occasionally. Turn ham and grill till done, 10 to 15 minutes more, brushing with sauce. Grill pineapple slices alongside the ham, brushing frequently with sauce. Place pineapple atop ham during last 5 to 10 minutes of grilling. Garnish with orange slices, if desired. 



Serves:
6
