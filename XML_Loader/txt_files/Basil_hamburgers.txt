Name:
Basil hamburgers
Type:
Meat
Country:
USA
Author:
http://recipes.chatelaine.com/
Ingredients:
1 lb ( 500 g) ground beef 
1 small onion, finely chopped 
1/4 cup ( 50 mL) fresh basil leaves, finely chopped 
1/2 tsp ( 2 mL) salt 
1/4 tsp ( 1 mL) ground black pepper  
1/4 tsp ( 1 mL) Tabasco 
4 hamburger buns 
cucumber salsa  

Instructions:
1. Oil grill and preheat barbecue. Place beef in a large mixing bowl. Add onion, basil and seasonings. Work meat with a fork or your hands until well mixed. Form into 4 burgers, about 1 inch (2.5 cm) thick. Place on barbecue and grill until done as you like, about 7 to 8 minutes per side for medium-well. Serve in our Old-fashioned hamburger buns (this recipe is also available in the Recipe File) topped with cucumber salsa. 

Nutrients per serving 
27.2 g protein, 4.3 mg iron, 15.8 g fat, 42.0 mg calcium, 45.5 g carbohydrates, 441.0 calories. 
Serves:
4
Notes:
Flavored with fresh basil, this superb juicy burger is adapted from New Maritime Seasonal Cooking (Nimbus) by Donna Young and Marg Routledge.
