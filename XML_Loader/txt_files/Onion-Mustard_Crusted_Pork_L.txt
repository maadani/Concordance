Name:
Onion-Mustard Crusted Pork L
Type:
Pork
Country:
USA
Author:
Great American Recipes
Ingredients:
2 pounds pork loin, trimmed of most of fat, boneless 
3 tablespoons Dijon-style mustard 
2 teaspoons soy sauce 
1 large clove garlic, minced 
1/2 onion, chopped 
1/2 teaspoon rosemary 
1 tablespoon olive oil 

Instructions:
1. Place pork roast on a roasting rack in shallow pan.
2. In a small bowl, combine the mustard, soy sauce, garlic, onion, rosemary and olive oil. Spread mixture over the entire roast.
3. Roast, uncovered, at 350 degrees for about 1 3/4 hours. Coat with any extra onion-mustard mixture during cooking.
4. Slice and serve.

Serves:
0
Notes:
Good served with oven-roasted potatoes or spicy rice, green beans and a tossed salad with vinaigrette dressing.
