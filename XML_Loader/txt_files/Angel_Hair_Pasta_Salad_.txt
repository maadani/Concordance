Name:
Angel Hair Pasta Salad 
Type:
Salads
Country:
USA
Author:
Kelly~WA
Ingredients:
2 (8 ounce) packages angel hair pasta
1/2 pound bay shrimp, cooked
3/4 cup chopped green onions
1 1/2 cups ranch style salad dressing
Instructions:
Cook the pasta according to package directions; drain and rinse under cold water.

Mix together the pasta, shrimp, green onions and ranch
dressing, then carefully work the shrimp into the pasta with your hands. (Note: As the salad sits, it will absorb some of the dressing; add a little dressing right before serving).
Serves:
6
