Name:
Andouille a la Jeannine
Type:
Appetizers
Country:
USA
Author:
Justin Wilson "Gourmet and Gourmand Cookbook"
Ingredients:
1 c Dry white wine 
2 lb Andouille or smoked sausage 
2 tb Honey 
1 tb Creole mustard 
Instructions:
Slice andouille 1/4-to 1/2-inch thick. Mix all liquid ingredients and pour over andouille in a covered skillet. Cook over low heat untill andouille is tender. Andouille is gumbo sausage for all you peoples who live away from the center of the universe. You can use other sausage and it would taste okay. 

Serves:
6
