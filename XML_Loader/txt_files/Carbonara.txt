Name:
Carbonara
Type:
Pizza
Country:
Norway
Author:
Sandi
Ingredients:
1 (12 inch) or 4 (6 inch) Boboli Italian bread shell 
5 ounces Boboli pizza sauce 
2 1/2 ounces bacon, cooked and crumbled 
2 ounces red onions, chopped 
4 teaspoons parsley, minced 
2 tablespoons parmesan cheese, grated 
 pepper to taste 

Instructions:
Layer ingredients on Boboli Italian bread shell in order given. Bake at 450 degrees for 12 -15 minutes (5-8 minutes for 6 inch shells)
