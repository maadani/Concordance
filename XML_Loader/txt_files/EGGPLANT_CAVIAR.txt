Name:
EGGPLANT CAVIAR
Type:
Side dish
Country:
Russia
Author:
Unknown (InterNet)
Ingredients:
1 medium eggplant
1 onion, chopped
1 clove garlic, minced
1/4 cup corn oil
1/4 cup tomato sauce
Salt, pepper
Black or white bread 


Instructions:
Pierce eggplant in several places around surface. Bake for 20-30 minutes until flesh is soft, or steam in small amount of water 20-30 min., or microwave on HIGH for 10- min. Cool, then peel and finely chop eggplant. 

Saute onion and garlic in oil until onion is tender. (Some avoid the garlic). Add eggplant, tomato sauce and season to taste with salt and pepper. Saute 5-10 min., stirring. Cool before serving. Serve as spread on black or white bread.  

Serves:
6
Notes:
Some people find offensive the presence of garlic, oil, and tomato sauce. (See Admonishment above, Relating to Garlic & Tomato Sauce!)
