Name:
Fruit Syrup Sauce - Saftsås
Type:
Deserts
Country:
Sweden
Author:
The Santesson Recipe Collection 
Ingredients:
Ingredients: 
2 dl fruit syrup, not too sweet 
2.5 dl water 
1.5 tablespoon potato starch (potato flour) 
honey as required 
Instructions:
Proceed as follows:
Mix the fruit syrup, water and potatoe starch and bring it to a boil while stirring constantly. 
Let it boil for 6 - 8 seconds and remove it from the heat. 
Taste and and honey as required. 
Serve either cold or above room temperature (but not hot). 


Serves:
0
Notes:
This is the standard sauce for "Ris à la Malta", rice with oranges and whipped cream
