Carrot candies
Type:
Candy
Country:

Author:
Unknown (InterNet)
Ingredients:
Ingredients: 

1 3/4 c Sugar 
3 tb -water 
1 lb Carrots; scraped and grated 
1 ts Ginger 
1/2 c Walnuts, finely chopped 
2 tb Lemon juice 

Instructions:
Dissolve the sugar in the water in a saucepan over low heat. Stir in the carrots, then bring to a boil. Boil for 30 minutes or until mixture is very thick. Remove from heat and stir in the giner, walnuts and the lemon juice. Pour into a greased square pan (possibly 9 inch?)Allow to cool and then cut into squares. When completely cool, remove from pan.
