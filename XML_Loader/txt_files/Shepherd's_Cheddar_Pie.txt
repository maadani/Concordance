Name:
Shepherd's Cheddar Pie
Type:
Pies
Country:
England
Author:
Campbell's Soup Company Ltd
Ingredients:
10 ounce can Campbell's Condensed Cheddar Cheese Soup 
3 cups hot mashed potatoes 
1 pound ground beef 
2 cups frozen mixed green beans, corn, peas, and carrots 
1 tablespoon Worcestershire sauce 
1/4 teaspoon ground black pepper 
1/4 teaspoon dried oregano leaves (optional) 

Instructions:
1. Make topping by combining 1/2 can soup with hot mashed potatoes; set aside.

2. In large non-stick frypan, brown ground beef at medium-high heat until cooked through. Drain excess fat.

3. Meanwhile, cook mixed vegetables according to package directions; add to frypan and stir into beef with remaining soup, Worcestershire sauce, pepper and oregano.

4. Spoon meat mixture into 8 x 8 x 2 inch square baking pan. Spread reserved potato mixture evenly over surface. Bake at 400 degrees for 20 minutes or until topping is light golden.

Serves:
4
