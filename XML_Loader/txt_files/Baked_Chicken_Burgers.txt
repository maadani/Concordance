Ingredients:
Ingredients 
************** 
1 cup chicken (cooked & cut up) 
1 tbsp. onion (minced) 
1 cup celery (chopped) 
1/4 tsp. salt 
1/2 cup processed american cheese 
1/8 tsp. pepper 
6 burger buns 
Butter 
1/4 cup mayonnaise



Instructions:
Procedure 
************* 
Heat oven to 350. In bowl, mix all ingredients except buns. Fill buns with chicken mixture. Wrap each sandwich in square of aluminum foil; place on baking sheet. Bake 20 minutes or till hot. Yield: 6 servings
