Name:
Fresh Cucumber Pickle (Ajad)
Type:
Sauces
Country:
Thailand
Author:
Thai Vegetarian Cooking by Vatcharin Bhumichitr
Ingredients:
4 tb Rice vinegar 
2 ts Sugar 
1/2 ts Salt 
1 oz Cucumber; finely chopped 
2 sm Shallots; finely chopped 
1 sm Carrot; finely chopped 
1 sm Red or green chili - finely chopped 
Instructions:
Mix all ingredients in a small bowl, stirring well until thoroughly mixed and serve. 
Serves:
3
