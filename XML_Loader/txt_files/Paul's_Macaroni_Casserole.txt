Name:
Paul's Macaroni Casserole
Type:
Cassoroles
Country:
USA
Author:
Great American Recipes
Ingredients:
1 1/2 cups elbow macaroni 
3/4 pound Italian sausage, hot or sweet 
1/3 cup onion, chopped 
1/4 cup green pepper, chopped 
1 large clove garlic, minced 
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano leaves 
1/4 teaspoon dried basil leaves 
8 ounces tomato sauce 
2 tablespoons chopped parsley, optional 
1 cup grated Mozzarella cheese 
1/2 cup Parmesan cheese 

Instructions:
1. Cook macaroni according to package directions, being careful not to overcook.

2. In the mean time, crumble the sausage into a large, heavy skillet.

3. Cook over medium heat until browned, about 10 minutes. Pour off some of the fat, leaving 1 to 2 tablespoons.

4. Stir in the onion, green pepper and garlic. Saute for 5 minutes until onion is soft.

5. Add oregano, basil, salt, pepper, tomato sauce, parsley, if used, cooked macaroni, Mozzarella cheese and 1/4 cup Parmesan cheese. Mix well.

6. Spoon mixture into a 2 quart casserole. Sprinkle with remaining Parmesan cheese.

7. Bake at 350 degrees for 20 to 25 minutes until hot.

Good served with: Romaine lettuce and sliced celery salad tossed with vinaigrette dressing, crusty bread and red wine, if desired.


Serves:
4
