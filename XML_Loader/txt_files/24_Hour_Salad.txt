Name:
24 Hour Salad
Type:
Salads
Country:
USA
Author:
Mabel Adams 
Ingredients:
Dressing
3 eggs -- beaten
4 tbl sugar
2 tbl vinegar
Salad
2 cups banana -- sliced
2 oranges -- cut up
2 cups marshmallows -- chopped*
2 cups pineapple -- drained
2 cups whipping cream
Instructions:
For dressing: Mix all ingred. Cook until it coats the spoon. Cool.
For Salad: Mix fruit together with whipping cream. Fold in the dressing. Refrigerate until serving time.
*I substitute mini-marshmallows 
Serves:
0
Notes:
This recipe came from an old family friend.
