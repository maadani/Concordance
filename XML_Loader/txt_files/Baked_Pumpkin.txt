Ingredients:
1 small pumpkin, peeled and cut into cubes 
1 cup sugar 
1 teaspoon salt 
Cinnamon


Instructions:
Place pumpkin cubes in a baking dish and sprinkle with sugar and salt. Cover pan with foil and bake in 325-degree oven until soft. Sprinkle with cinnamon.
