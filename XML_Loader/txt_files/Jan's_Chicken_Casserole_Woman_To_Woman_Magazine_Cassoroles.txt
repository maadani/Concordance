Name:
Jan's Chicken Casserole
Type:
Cassoroles
Country:
USA
Author:
Woman To Woman Magazine
Ingredients:
Amount Ingredient 
2 large whole chicken breasts 
4 slices Swiss cheese 
10 ounce can cream of chicken soup 
1/4 cup dry white wine 
1/2 package seasoned stuffing mix 
3 tablespoons melted butter 
  Paprika 

Instructions:
Skin and debone chicken breasts; cut in half; place in shallow casserole dish; Place one slice of cheese on top of each piece of chicken.
Blend soup and wine; pour over chicken; top with stuffing mix; drizzle with melted butter.
Bake at 350 degrees, uncovered, for 45 minutes to 1 hour.
