Name:
MAYONNAISE, RUSSIAN STYLE
Type:
Mayonnaise
Country:
Russia
Author:
Mrs. Andrew Shoopinsky
Ingredients:
3 egg yolks
1 tsp. Russian mustard (see above
recipe)
1/2 tsp. sugar
1/2 tsp. salt
Juice of 1/4 lemon
Wesson oil
1 Tbs. sour cream
1/2 jar capers, drained (optional)

Instructions:
Combine egg yolks, mustard, sugar, salt and lemon juice. Add a few sprinkles of cold water and, while beating, enough Wesson oil to reach desired consistency. At last minute, add sour cream and (optional) capers.
