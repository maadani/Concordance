Name:
Polvo Frito - Fried Octopus
Type:
Sea Foods
Country:
Portugal
Author:
Meryl Constance
Ingredients:
1 kg Octopus 
3 Eggs beaten 
1/4 c Milk 
Instructions:
salt and pepper to taste finely chopped parsley olive oil 
A friend born in Portugal supplied this simple and excellent example of Portuguese home-cooking. 
Precook 1 kg of octopus, drain and cool Cut it up into generous chunks. Beat 3 eggs, with a little milk salt and pepper to taste, and half a bunch of very finely chopped parsley. Coat the octopus pieces in the egg mixture and shallow fry in a little olive oil, turning them until are golden
Serves:
6
