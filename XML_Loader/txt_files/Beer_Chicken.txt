Name:
Beer Chicken
Type:
Poultry
Country:
USA
Author:
Great American Recipes
Ingredients:
1 chicken, 3 pounds, cut-up 
2 tablespoons vegetable oil 
1 cup chopped red onion 
1/2 cup chopped celery 
2 cloves garlic, minced 
1 can beer 
Dash salt 
Dash pepper 

Instructions:
1. Rinse chicken pieces with cool water. Pat dry with paper towels.
2. In a large skillet, heat the oil.
3. Brown chicken pieces on all sides.
4. Sprinkle chopped onion, celery, garlic, salt and pepper over chicken.
5. Add enough beer to barely cover chicken.
6. Cover. Simmer for 30 minutes or until chicken is done to your liking. Juices should run clear when pierced with a fork.
7. Serve hot with extra celery sprinkled over the top.
Serves:
4
