Name:
Glazed Pork Chops Dijonnaise
Type:
Pork
Country:
USA
Author:
Best Recipe Collection
Ingredients:
1/2 cup Hellmann's Dijonnaise mustard 
1/3 cup apricot or peach jam 
3 tablespoons sliced green onions 
2 teaspoons soy sauce 
1 tablespoon Mazola corn oil 
4 - 6 boneless pork loin chops (about 1 pound) 

Instructions:
In small bowl combine mustard, jam, green onions and soy sauce; set aside. In large skillet heat oil over medium-high heat; brown pork chops about 4 minutes on each side or until cooked through. Set aside and keep warm. Drain fat from skillet. Pour mustard mixture into skillet; cook and stir over low heat until slightly thickened. Return pork chops to skillet; turning to coat. Heat through. 


Serves:
4
Notes:
This sauce is also excellent on chicken. Substitute pork with boneless, skinless chicken breasts.
