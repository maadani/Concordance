Name:
Breakfast Sausage Patties
Type:
Pork
Country:
USA
Author:
Great American Recipes
Ingredients:
1 1/2 pounds ground pork 
1/3 cup green onion, finely chopped 
2 teaspoons fennel seeds 
1/2 teaspoon paprika 
2 tablespoons fresh rosemary, finely chopped 
1 tablespoon fresh oregano leaves, finely chopped 
2 cloves garlic, minced 
1 tablespoon minced parsley 
1 to 1 1/2 teaspoons salt 
1/2 teaspoon pepper 
1/2 teaspoon crushed red pepper 
1 tablespoon oil 

Instructions:
1. Mix together pork, green oniion, fennel seeds, paprika, rosemary, oregano, garlic parsley, salt, pepper, and curshed red pepper. Blend well.
2. Refrigerate overnight.
3. Remove mixture from refrigerator. Form into 3 inch patties.
4. Saute patties in hot oil until very brown and crisp on both sides, about 10 minutes.
5. Serve immediately with pancakes and maple syrup.
