Name:
Rock Cakes
Type:
Cakes
Country:
Australia
Author:
Unknown (Internet)
Ingredients:
2 cups self raising flour 
1/4 tsp mixed spice 
1/3 cup butter 
1/3 cup caster sugar 
2 Tbsp sultanas 
2 Tbsp currants 
1 Tbsp mixed peel 
1 egg, beaten 
1/2 cup milk 

Instructions:
Preheat oven to 220C (425F). Sift flour and spice. Rub butter into flour with fingertips. Add sugar, fruit and peel. Mix with egg and sufficient milk to form a stiff dough. Place spoonfuls of mixture on greased oven tray (cookie sheet). Bake at 200C until golden brown, 10 - 15 minutes. Cool on wire rack.

Serves:
24
