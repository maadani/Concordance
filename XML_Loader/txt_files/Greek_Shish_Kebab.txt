Name:
Greek Shish Kebab
Type:
Lamb
Country:
Greece
Author:
Recipe Card from Safeway
Ingredients:
1 tablespoon olive oil or vegetable oil 
3 tablespoons lemon juice 
1 small onion, finely chopped 
1 clove garlic, minced 
2 bay leaves 
2 teaspoons oregano leaves 
1/2 teaspoon black pepper 
2 pounds lean boneless lamb, fat removed, cut in 1 1/2 inch cubes 
1 large mild red onion, cut into 1 1/2 inch pieces 
1 large green or red bell pepper, seeded and cut in 1 1/2 inch squares 
1/2 pound medium mushroo 

Instructions:
Combine oil, lemon juice, chopped onion, garlic, bay leaves, oregano and black pepper. Add lamb; stir to coat. Cover and refrigerate for at least 4 hours, stirring several times. Remove meat, reserving marinade. Thread meat, red onion, bell pepper and mushrooms on skewers. Preheat broiler; place shish kebabs on rack in pan, about 3 inches from heat. Broil, turning and basting occasionally with marinade, until meat and vegetables are well browned but meat is still pink in center, 10 to 15 minutes total.
