Name:
Turkish Delight 3
Type:
Candy
Country:
Greece
Author:
Daphne Metaxas
Ingredients:
3 tb Unflavored gelatine 
1/2 c Cold water 
1/3 c Boiling water 
2 c Sugar 
4 tb Extract * 
Food coloring ** 
1/4 c Pistachio nuts; chopped 
1 c Confectioners' sugar 
Instructions:
Soften gelatine in cold water. Add sugar to boiling water. Bring to boil. Stir in softened gelatine. Simmer, uncovered, 20 minutes. Add flavoring and coloring. Pour into ungreased, 8-inch square pan. Add nuts. Stir gently. Chill. When firm, invert onto cutting board. Cut into 1 1/2 inch cubes. Roll in confectioners' sugar. Store in a jar at room temperature. Serve with Greek Coffee.
