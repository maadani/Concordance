Name:
Steamed Jasmine Rice - Khao Suay 
Type:
Vegetarian
Country:
Thailand
Author:
Thailand The Beautiful Cookbook
Ingredients:
3 c Jasmine Rice 
3 c Water 
Instructions:
Place rice in a large saucepan. Rinse twice to clean the rice, draining thoroughly. Add the water to the rice. 
Cover the saucepan and heat to boiling. Allow to boil on high heat for 1 minute. Turn the temperature to low and steam for 10 minutes. Reduce the heat to the lowest setting and allow to steam for 10 minutes more.
Serves:
4
Notes:
The wonderful aroma and subtle flavor of jasmine rice compliment every dish perfectly. Thais cook rice almost instinctively - it is their staple food
