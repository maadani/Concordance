Name:
Pork Chow Mein
Type:
Pork
Country:
USA
Author:
Vincent Posella
Ingredients:
1 cup minced cooked pork 
1/2 small chopped onion 
1 minced celery stalk 
1/2 cup shredded cabbage 
1/2 cup bean sprouts 
1 cup chow mein noodles 
2 tablespoons soya sauce 
1/2 cup chicken stock made with dissolved Oxo cube or 1 tablespoon chicken base 
1 tablespoon oil or bacon grease 

Instructions:
Stir fry the vegetables. Add soya sauce, pork meat, chicken stock and chow mein noodles.
Serves:
4

null
