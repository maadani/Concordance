Ingredients:
1 small package instant butterscotch pudding mix 
1 (8 oz.) carton cool whip 
1 (8 oz.) can crushed pineapple 
1 cup mini marshmallows 
3 cups chopped apples with peels 
1 cup dry roasted peanuts 
Instructions:
Mix dry pudding, cool whip and pineapple and juice. Stir in remaining ingredients. Then refrigerate
