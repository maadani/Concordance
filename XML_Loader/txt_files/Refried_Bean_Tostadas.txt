Ingredients:
Ingredients 
1 3/4 cups (1-pound can) ORTEGA® Refried Beans 
1/4 cup chopped onion 
1 package (1.25 ounces) ORTEGA® Taco Seasoning Mix 
1 package (10) ORTEGA® Tostada Shells, warmed 
2 cups shredded lettuce 
1/2 cup (2 ounces) SARGENTO® Fancy Shredded Mild Cheddar Cheese 
1/3 cup sliced ripe olives 
2 medium ripe avocados,, cut into 20 slices 
3/4 cup ORTEGA® Thick & Smooth Taco Sauce 

Instructions:
Directions
COMBINE beans, onion and seasoning mix in medium saucepan. Cook, stirring frequently, for 4 to 5 minutes or until heated through.

SPREAD 1/4 cup bean mixture over each shell. Top with lettuce, cheese, olives, avocado and taco sauce.
