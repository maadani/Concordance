Name:
Pork Chops with Sauce
Type:
Pork
Country:
USA
Author:
Rival Manufacturing
Ingredients:
4 to 8 pork chops 
  Salt 
  Pepper 
Sauces: Choose one: 
10 3/4 ounce can cream of mushroom soup 
8 ounce jar sweet and sour sauce 
10 3/4 ounce can cream of chicken soup 
10 3/4 ounce can chicken and rice soup 
1 1/2 cups barbecue sauce 

Instructions:
Brown pork chops well and season lightly with salt and pepper. Place in crock pot and cover with any one of the sauces above. Cover and cook on Low 7 to 9 hours (High 4 to 5 hours).
