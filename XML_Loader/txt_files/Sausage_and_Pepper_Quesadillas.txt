Name:
Sausage and Pepper Quesadillas
Type:
Pork
Country:
Canada
Author:
Canadian Living Magazi
Ingredients:
8 large flour tortillas 
1 pound Italian sausage 
1 sweet green pepper, chopped 
1 1/2 cups meatless pasta sauce 
1 1/2 cups shredded mozzarella cheese 

Instructions:
In skillet, cook sausage over medium-high heat, breaking up with wooden spoon, for 5 minutes or until no longer pink; drain. Stir in green pepper; cook for 5 minutes or until tender crisp. Stir in pasta sauce.

Place tortillas on work surface. Spoon filling on half of each tortilla. Sprinkle filling with cheese; fold tortilla over top.

Bake on baking sheet in 400 degree oven for 10 minutes per side OR cook in skillet over medium heat for 3 minutes per side OR grill over medium heat for 4 minutes per side or until golden and cheese is melted

Serves:
4
