Name:
Cocoon Chicken
Type:
Poultry
Country:
USA
Author:
Great American Recipes
Ingredients:
1 chicken, cut up 
1 egg 
1 teaspoon dried oregano leaves 
1/4 teaspoon salt 
1/4 teaspoon pepper 
6 strips lean bacon 
Seasoned Bread Crumbs   
1 cup dried, fine bread crumbs 
1/2 teaspoon dried oregano leaves 
1/2 teaspoon garlic powder 
1/2 teaspoon parsley or summer savory 
  Salt, to taste, optional 
  Pepper, to taste, optional 

Instructions:
1. Wash chicken pieces. Pat dry.
2. Beat egg with oregano, salt and pepper. Set aside.
3. Mix all ingredients for the seasoned bread crumbs. Toss until well blended. Place on a plate or flat bowl.
4. Roll a strip of bacon around each piece of chicken in a spiral. Secure ends with a toothpick if necessary.
5. Dip the chicken pieces into the seasoned bread crumbs, making sure that all sides are coated.
6. Place chicken pieces in a roasting pan.
7. Bake at 375 degrees for about 45 minutes, or until the juices run clear when pierced with a fork.
8. Remove the toothpicks. Serve.

Good served with: Red potatoes with butter and parsley, steamed zucchini and a chilled cucumber salad.
