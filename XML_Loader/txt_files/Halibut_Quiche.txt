Name:
Halibut Quiche
Type:
Side dish
Country:
Russia
Author:
Ella Woodhead
Ingredients:
1/2 cup mayonnaise 
2 Tbsp. all purpose flour 
2 eggs beaten 
1/2 cup milk 
2 cups flaked halibut (boiled and drained) 
1/3 cup chopped green onions 
1 Tbsp. finely chopped parsley 
2 cups shredded Swiss cheese 
1 unbaked pastry shell 

Instructions:
Combine mayonnaise, flour, eggs and milk in a bowl. Gently stir in halibut, onion, parsley and cheese. Spoon into the pastry shell and bake at 350 degrees for one hour.
