Name:
Beer Bread
Type:
Breads
Country:
USA
Author:
allenm@sna.com
Ingredients:
1 12 oz (336 grm). beer 
2 tbsp (30 ml) sugar 
3 cups (700 ml) self-rising flour
Instructions:
Mix all. 
Pour in greased pan. 
Press down with the back of a wet spoon. 
Oil the top. 
Bake at 350 degrees (175 C.) for 1 hr and 20 min.
