Name:
Mom's Spanish Rice
Type:
Cassoroles
Country:
Spain
Author:
Mom
Ingredients:
2 tb Salad oil 
1 1/3 c Rice 
2 tb Minced onion 
1 lb Ground beef/chuck/hamburger 
1/2 ts Salt 
2 8 oz cans tomato sauce 
1 Beef bouillon cube 
1 c Hot water 
1 ts Soy sauce 
1 ts Salt 
Instructions:
In hot fat in skillet, cook rice with onion and beef till golden brown. Add salt and rest of ingredients; simmer, covered until rice is tender
Serves:
4

null
