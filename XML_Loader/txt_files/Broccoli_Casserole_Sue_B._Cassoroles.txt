Name:
Broccoli Casserole
Type:
Cassoroles
Country:
USA
Author:
Sue B.
Ingredients:
2 packs chopped broccoli 
1 can cream of mushroom soup 
1/2 cup mayo 
1 egg 
1/4 small onion 
  Pepperidge Farms stuffing mix 
  Cheddar cheese 

Instructions:
Cook broccoli. Combine soup, mayo, and egg. Mix with broccoli and chopped onion. Grease casserole; pour mix into casserole; sprinkle with cheddar cheese. Top with stuffing mix. Bake for 35 to 40 minutes at 350 degrees.
Serves:
6
