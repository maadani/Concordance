Name:
Chicken in its own juice (Pollo en su Jugo)
Type:
Poultry
Country:
Mexico
Author:
http://mexico.udg.mx
Ingredients:
1 large chicken cut into pieces 
2 cups of chicken broth 
4 tablespoons of vinegar 
4 onion, sliced 
thyme 
oil 
salt 
Instructions:
Wash the chicken well and dry it. Put salt on the chicken and fry it in hot oil. Remove the chicken and fry the onion in the same oil. 
Put the chicken back in the pot and add the vinegar, thyme and salt. Cover the pot and cook until tender. Serve with lettuce or cooked vegetables.
