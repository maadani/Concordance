Name:
Spareribs with Honey-Mustard Garlic Sauce
Type:
Pork
Country:
United Arab Emirates
Author:
Recipes Plus Calendar
Ingredients:
3 pounds pork spareribs, cut in 3 - 4 rib pieces 
1 bottle Wong Wing honey-mustard garlic sauce 
3 tablespoons Wong Wing soya sauce 
2 tablespoons ketchup 

Instructions:
In large saucepan of boiling water cook ribs, covered, 45 minutes. Drain. Place ribs in roasting pan.
Mix together honey-mustard garlic sauce, soya sauce and ketchup; pour over ribs.
Cover and bake at 350 degrees 30 minutes, rotating ribs every 15 minutes. Uncover and bake 15 minutes longer or until tender.
Serves:
6
