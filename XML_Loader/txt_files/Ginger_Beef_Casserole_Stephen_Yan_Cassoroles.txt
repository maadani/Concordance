Name:
Ginger Beef Casserole
Type:
Cassoroles
Country:
USA
Author:
Stephen Yan
Ingredients:
1 pound beef meat, sliced 
4 green onions, cut into 2" lengths 
1 tablespoon ginger, shredded 
1/2 teaspoon minced garlicr 
4 ounces water chestnuts, sliced 
1/4 cup onion, shredded 
Sauce:  
1/4 teaspoon salt 
2 tablespoons oyster sauce 
1 teaspoon sugar 
1/4 teaspoon gourmet powder (M.S.G.) 
1 tablespoon dark soy sauce 
a few drops sesame seed oil 
1/3 cup water 
1 1/2 tablespoons Wonder Powder (very fine white tapioca powder) 
1 tablespoon Chinese cooking wine 

Instructions:
Marinate beef with 1 teaspoon Wonder Powder, 2 tablespoons light soy sauce, 1 tablespoon cooking wine and drops of sesame oil.
Heat wok, brown green onions, garlic, onion, and ginger.
Add beef and saute until meat changes color.
Add sauce and bring to a boil.
Transfer to a heat-proof casserole.
Heat casserole until sauce comes to a boil.
Serve from the casserole while sauce is still bubbling.
